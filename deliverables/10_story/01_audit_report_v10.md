Pass as the current standard-format export candidate.

v10 fixes the v9 structural blocker: the script no longer reads like a short treatment expanded into feature runtime. The scene chain is now dense enough to carry a long animation feature workflow: first exchange evidence, school/home/work consequences, relationship traces, search denial, rescue logistics, and adult near-misses all exist as separate shootable scenes.

This audit does not claim the uploaded PDF is official or that the draft copies it. The comparison standard here is functional quality: length, structure, scene rhythm, causality, and production executability.

## Remaining Non-Blocking Differences

1. The uploaded PDF remains much longer in extracted raw lines, but its formatting and fan-made status make line count a weaker target than scene function density.
2. v10 is still a clean functional screenplay rather than a fully polished final dialogue draft. It is suitable for standard screenplay DOCX export and further human review.
3. If another pass is desired after reading the DOCX, the highest-value polish is not more plot; it is central-pair dialogue texture and scene-level emotional silence.
