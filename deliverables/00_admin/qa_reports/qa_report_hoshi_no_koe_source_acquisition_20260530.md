## Comparison Policy

Until a licensed copy of the DVD storyboard, DVD BOOK storyboard, or authorized screenplay/transcript is provided, comparison must stay at the non-reproductive level:

- emotional engine
- value movement
- scene function
- object/motif function
- escalation shape
- time-delay mechanics
- ending function
- runtime density

Do not compare by copying or reconstructing original dialogue, shot order, character names, ship names, enemy terms, or exact scene wording.
