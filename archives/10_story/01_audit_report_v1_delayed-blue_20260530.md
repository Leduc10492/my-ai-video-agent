# 《迟来的蓝光》v1 McKee Audit

## Result

Pass with revision needed. The draft has a clear emotional spine and a strong object motif, but the protagonist's active pursuit arrives too late. v2 should bring Lin Chuan's choice pressure earlier, sharpen Xu Cheng's offscreen danger into a visible cost, and make the final send feel like an irreversible action rather than only an acceptance beat.

## Story Spine Check

| Element | Evaluation |
| --- | --- |
| Inciting incident | Present: Xu Cheng announces her selection for the deep-space mapping mission. It breaks the relationship equilibrium. |
| Progressive complications | Present but mostly passive: message delay increases, Lin Chuan receives delayed voice notes, failed replies accumulate. |
| Crisis | Present: he receives a seven-year-old message and must decide whether to send a reply with no calculable return. |
| Climax | Present but quiet: he sends the reply. Needs a sharper irreversible cost or action. |
| Resolution | Present: failed receipt, static, repair work resumes. Good tonal fit. |

## Top Issues

1. Issue: Protagonist agency is delayed.
   Evidence: Lin Chuan mainly receives, listens, deletes, and waits until the repair-shop scene.
   Impact: The final choice is emotionally legible but structurally late.
   Severity: P1
   Fix: Add an earlier action where he tries to build or alter a receiver/transmitter, then fails. This makes his final send a reversal of control.

2. Issue: Xu Cheng's danger is abstract.
   Evidence: The deep-space scene has a warning light and ice-dust alarm, but no consequence beyond interruption.
   Impact: The distance is clear, but the cost of her path is not fully dramatized.
   Severity: P2
   Fix: Make the alarm damage the blue radio or cost her a personal recording, tying danger to the shared object.

3. Issue: The middle repeat pattern risks flattening.
   Evidence: Several beats use receive/listen/delete/continue.
   Impact: The waiting mood is strong, but repeated internal hesitation could slow the short.
   Severity: P2
   Fix: Convert one deletion beat into an external scene with another person or object that pressures Lin Chuan to act.

4. Issue: Theme is stated slightly directly.
   Evidence: "我们只是把一小段自己，放到很远的地方。希望有人收到。"
   Impact: Effective, but close to thesis speech.
   Severity: P3
   Fix: Let an action carry more of this meaning; reduce the line or pair it with a visible incomplete transmission.

## Revision Priorities

1. Add a receiver/transmitter build attempt before the final crisis.
2. Tie Xu Cheng's deep-space risk to the blue radio.
3. Replace one passive waiting beat with a paid repair-shop subplot involving a child and a broken TV.
4. Trim one thematic line from Xu Cheng's final message.

## Downstream Impact

If revised, downstream guides should preserve:

- Blue radio as recurring prop.
- Seaside repair shop as Earth-side anchor.
- Blue voice-message UI as communication motif.
- Deep-space survey pod as counterpart location.
