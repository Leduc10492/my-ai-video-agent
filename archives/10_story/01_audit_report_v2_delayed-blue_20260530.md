# 《迟来的蓝光》v2 McKee / Screenwriter Audit

## Legal Source Status

Official public pages confirm the premise, staff, length, and DVD/video-storyboard product information for `Voices of a Distant Star`. No official free full screenplay text was found in this pass. Because the original screenplay is copyrighted, this audit compares craft dimensions rather than text, dialogue, or scene-by-scene replication.

## Result

v2 is materially stronger than v1. It now has earlier protagonist agency, a visible midpoint cost, and an irreversible climax. The draft is not yet final because it is estimated above target runtime and the final message can be compressed further.

## Top Issues

1. Issue: Runtime is above target.
   Evidence: v2 estimates about 10:35 against an 8-10 minute target.
   Impact: The script may feel a little over-articulated for a short proof-of-work.
   Severity: P1
   Fix: Compress the station scene and failed-reply shore scene into fewer beats; keep the child TV subplot.

2. Issue: Final message is still slightly explanatory.
   Evidence: "你那个空信号，如果还在发，我会偶尔听一下" states acceptance clearly.
   Impact: The idea is good, but the action of listening to static can carry more weight.
   Severity: P2
   Fix: Cut or shorten this line; let the burned receiver and morning static do the work.

3. Issue: Xu Cheng's arc remains mostly symbolic.
   Evidence: Her scenes are strong but sparse; she loses the radio but has no second tactical action.
   Impact: The Earth-side protagonist dominates, which is acceptable for this version but less balanced than a two-voice romance.
   Severity: P2
   Fix: Add one small action where she chooses to send the damaged/partial message instead of preserving power, if runtime allows.

## Recommended v3 Pass

1. Cut 35-60 seconds by compressing repeated receive/listen beats.
2. Make Xu Cheng's final partial message more fragmented and less thesis-like.
3. Keep the final transmitter burnout.
4. Preserve all original names and settings; do not move closer to the copyrighted original.

## Current Quality Verdict

Quality gap is moderate-low on structure and motif, but not yet low enough for final DOCX export. One more Screenwriter pass should bring the draft into a tighter, more filmable 8-9 minute version.
