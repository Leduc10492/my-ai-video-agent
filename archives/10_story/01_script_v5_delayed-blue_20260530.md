## Source And Copyright Boundary

Public reference facts used for this workflow test:

- CoMix Wave describes `Voices of a Distant Star` as a story about close school friends separated when one leaves for a space mission; they keep in touch while distance makes delivery slower.
- CoMix Wave lists the film length as roughly 24 minutes.
- This draft is an original script using a comparable craft problem: intimacy under cosmic-scale communication delay.

This is not a remake, transcript, translation, subtitle reconstruction, or scene-order reconstruction. It uses original characters, setting, object motif, conflict mechanics, and ending action.
